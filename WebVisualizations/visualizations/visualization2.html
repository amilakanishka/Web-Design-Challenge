City Latitude vs Humidity

Ther purpose of this projectwas to analyze how weather changes as you get closer to the equator. To accomplish this analysis, we first pulled data from the OpenWeatherMap API to assesmble a dataset on over 500 cities.

After assembling the dataset, we used Matplotlib to plot various aspects of the weather vs. latitude. Factors we looked at included: temperature, cloudness, wind speed, and humidity. This siteprovides the source data and visualizations created as part of the analysis, we well as expectations and descriptions of any trends and correlations witnessed.
